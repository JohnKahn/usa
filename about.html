About Us

Founded as Rudolph Enterprises in 2003, USA Restaurant Services is redefining what it means to be a restaurant services provider. With our fixed-prices, reduced repair rates, and cutting edge technologies, we have achieved an unprecedented 98% approval rating while managing over 145 restaurants doing $190 million in annual revenue. In August of 2017, USA Restaurant Services merged with USA Restaurant Suppliers, one of the fastest growing used restaurant suppliers in the industry in order to provide customers the simplest and most efficient way to renovate or build new restaurants. We’ve built a team of industry leading repair technicians, developers, and client-relationship experts who work together to provide restaurant's exceptional support on their road to success.
